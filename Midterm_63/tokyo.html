About Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and one of the most populous cities in the world. It is known for its modern skyscrapers, bustling streets, and vibrant pop culture.

Things to Do

Some popular attractions in Tokyo include:

  • Visiting the Tokyo Skytree
  • Exploring the historic Asakusa district
  • Strolling through the gardens at the Imperial Palace
  • Shopping in the trendy Shibuya district
